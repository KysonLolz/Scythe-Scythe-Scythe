About Rowan

Rowan Damisch evolves from a quiet and overlooked teenager into a courageous and determined individual. His journey in the Scythedom reveals his struggle with morality, power, and identity, ultimately shaping him into a figure who challenges the status quo.

Key Facts

  • Role: Apprentice under Scythe Faraday
  • Known For: Questioning authority and justice
  • Arc: From novice to challenging the Scythedom's corrupt practices
