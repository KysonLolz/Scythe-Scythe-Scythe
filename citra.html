About Citra

Citra Terranova is a strong-willed and intelligent character whose journey in the Scythedom showcases her determination and compassion. From her beginnings as a regular teenager to becoming a Scythe, she consistently challenges the morality of her world and strives to make it better.

Key Traits

  • Brave and resourceful
  • Morally driven
  • Compassionate yet firm
