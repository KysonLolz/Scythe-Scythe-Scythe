Scythe Faraday

About Scythe Faraday

Scythe Faraday represents the moral and ethical foundation of the Scythedom. Known for his wisdom, restraint, and fairness, he adheres to the original ideals of the Scythedom, ensuring that his actions are always just. As a mentor to Rowan and Citra, he instills in them the values necessary for the noble role of a scythe.

Key Facts

  • Role: Mentor to Rowan and Citra
  • Philosophy: Adheres to the original Scythe commandments
  • Known For: Wisdom, fairness, and strict adherence to ethics
