Scythe Goddard

About Scythe Goddard

Scythe Goddard is a charismatic yet ruthless figure within the Scythedom. Known for his cruelty and manipulation, he represents a dangerous faction that seeks to overhaul the Scythedom's original ideals. His ambition and lust for power place him in direct opposition to characters like Scythe Faraday, Rowan, and Citra.

Key Facts

  • Role: Scythe and Leader of a radical faction
  • Known For: Ruthlessness, manipulation, and power hunger
  • Motivation: To challenge the old ways of the Scythedom
